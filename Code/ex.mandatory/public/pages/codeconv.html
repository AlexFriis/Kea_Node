Coding Conventions

Here's a list of the coding conventions i try sticking to:

Keeping a good code standard ensures quality and readability which directly translates to better maintainability.

  • Proper naming and declaration.
  • Descriptive naming and declaration.
  • Keep It Simple Stupid - Avoid confusion.
  • Proper spacing for operators 1+1 is harder to read than 1 + 1.
  • Statement rules - Always end a statement with a semicolon.
  • Keep closing brackets on the same line to ensure readability.


Side note - Every developer has their own unique coding-style and biases towards specific coding conventions.
What one party may find important may be completely irrelevant to another party.
Therefore a common understanding of codepractices is usually discussed among groups of programmers to ensure everyone is on the same page.
